# Financial_Dashboard

## Objective :
You are working as a Data Analyst at The Finance Group. The client has requested a monthlylevel interactive dashboard in Power BI to monitor the company's financial health and key KPIs. Your task is to design and deliver a dashboard that allows filtering by date range and other key business metrics. 

### Requirements :
* Monthly P&L Data (Revenue, COGS, Gross Profit, Opex, EBITDA) 
* Cash Flow Data (Inflows, Outflows, Net Cash) 
* Sales Data (Product/Service, Region, Month) 
* Receivables & Payables Aging 
* Budget vs Actuals

### Dashboard Scope: 
* Time-Period Selector: Filter by month/quarter/year
* KPI Cards: Revenue, Gross Margin %, EBITDA %, Net Cash
* Visuals: Revenue & Profit Trend, Budget vs Actual variance %, Product/Service performance, Receivables Aging, Cash Flow Waterfall 

### Interactivity: 
* Filters for region, product category, and date range
* Drill-downs (e.g., click a month to see category-level data)

### Data Accuracy: 
* Clean and document missing or incorrect data
